About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

Food Funday is a team work of Food Funday Restaurant Cafe, we aim at promoting the foodstuff industry through the branches , we establish and through the new dishes. and integrate between different cultures.

We aim to home-produce as much as possiblefor the best quality, and to reduce food miles. Our delicious cakes, traditional Gujarati Dishes, breads, soups, sauces and accompaniments are produced in our own kitchens – and we use herbs from our gardens when in season. Even our bottled water is produced in-house, using a sophisticated seven-stage filtration.

Our Menu

We can make a commitment to promote vegetables and fruits and whole grains on every part of every menu. We can make portion sizes smaller and emphasize quality over quantity. And we can help create a culture - imagine this - where our kids ask for healthy options instead of resisting them.
